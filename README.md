## Project Overview

Sales prediction means estimating how much of a product people will buy based on advertising investments. Businesses spend money on different advertising platforms such as TV, Radio, and Newspaper to promote their products. Predicting sales helps companies plan their marketing strategies effectively.

In this project, Machine Learning and Data Visualization techniques are used to analyze advertising data and predict product sales.

---

## Dataset

The dataset used in this project is the **Advertising Dataset** downloaded from Kaggle.

### Dataset Columns

* **TV** – Advertising budget spent on TV.
* **Radio** – Advertising budget spent on Radio.
* **Newspaper** – Advertising budget spent on Newspaper.
* **Sales** – Sales of the product.

---

## Objectives

* Analyze advertising budgets across different platforms.
* Visualize relationships between advertising spend and sales.
* Understand correlations between variables.
* Predict future sales using machine learning models.

## Data Analysis and Visualization

In this project, several visualizations were created to understand the dataset:

* Sales distribution histogram
* TV advertising vs Sales scatter plot
* Radio advertising vs Sales scatter plot
* Newspaper advertising vs Sales scatter plot
* Correlation heatmap
* Pairplot visualization
* Advertising budget comparison
* Pie chart of advertising distribution
* Sales trend line chart
* Moving average analysis

These charts help understand how advertising affects product sales.

---

## Machine Learning Model

A **Linear Regression model** is used to predict sales based on advertising budgets.

Steps involved:

1. Load the dataset
2. Data preprocessing
3. Train-test split
4. Train Linear Regression model
5. Predict sales
6. Visualize prediction results
